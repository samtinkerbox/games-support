Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

SamTinkerBox ("we", "our", "us") operates several mobile games including Italian Brainrot: Merge, Brainrot Hunter, and Iromaze (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically collected

  • Device information: device model, OS version, language
  • Usage data: game progress (stored locally on your device)
  • Advertising identifiers: IDFA (iOS) or Google Advertising ID (Android) via Google AdMob, used for ads

We do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or address
  • Contacts, photos, or location
  • Any account registration information

2. How We Use Information

  • To show ads via Google AdMob
  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To understand app performance and crashes

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data as described in their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

Game progress (high scores, discovered monsters, saved colors, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload this data to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized ads: In iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking, or Android Settings > Google > Ads
  • Reset your advertising ID: In device settings
  • Delete app data: Uninstall the app

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.
